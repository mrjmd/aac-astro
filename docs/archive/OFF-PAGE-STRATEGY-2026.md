# Attack A Crack: 2026 Off-Site Strategy (Beyond the Website)

*Prepared: March 2026*

---

## Executive Summary

While the website is the foundation, a successful 2026 local SEO strategy requires a comprehensive off-site presence. This document outlines the external signals (social, PR, local citations, and offline marketing) needed to reinforce the Attack A Crack brand entity.

## 1. Social Media & Content Distribution

**Goal:** Establish active trust signals and reach local homeowners on platforms they use daily.
**Cadence:** Weekly (minimum)

### Primary Platform: Facebook & Instagram (Meta)
- **Why:** Homeowners 35-65+ are heavily active here.
- **Strategy:**
    - **Before/After Showcases:** Post weekly "Transformation Tuesday" style posts.
    - **Local Community Groups:** Monitor local town groups (e.g., "Everything Quincy", "Simsbury Neighbors") for keywords like "crack", "basement", "water". Reply helpfully, not just spamming.
    - **Video Reels:** 15-30 second clips of epoxy injection or concrete resurfacing. Visuals sell this service.
    - **Tagging:** Always tag the specific town/location in posts to signal local relevance to algorithms.

### Secondary Platform: YouTube
- **Why:** Video is the #1 trust signal in 2026. YouTube videos often rank in Google search results.
- **Strategy:**
    - **"How It Works" Library:** 2-3 minute videos explaining the process (Injection, Staples, Resurfacing).
    - **Customer Testimonials:** Video interviews with happy clients.
    - **SEO Optimization:** Use detailed descriptions with local keywords and timestamps.

### Tertiary Platform: LinkedIn
- **Why:** Professional networking for B2B referrals (Property Managers, Realtors).
- **Strategy:**
    - **Partner Networking:** Connect with local realtors, home inspectors, and insurance adjusters.
    - **Professional Updates:** Share company milestones, certifications, and educational content about property value.

## 2. Local Authority & Backlink Building

**Goal:** Earn high-quality, locally relevant backlinks that prove we are a legitimate New England business.
**Metric:** 1-2 high-quality local links per month.

### 2.1 The "Hyper-Local" approach
- **Little League / Youth Sports Sponsorships:**
    - **Cost:** Low ($300-$500/season).
    - **Benefit:** Often gets a link from a `.org` or local domain (high trust).
    - **Action:** Sponsor one team in each major hub (Quincy, Hartford, etc.).
- **Local Chamber of Commerce:**
    - **Action:** Join chambers in key target cities. Ensure profile has a do-follow link.
- **Local News / Patch.com:**
    - **Action:** Submit press releases for company milestones (e.g., "Local Business Celebrates 30 Years", "New Eco-Friendly Repair Method").

### 2.2 Industry Partnerships (B2B)
- **Home Inspectors:**
    - **Strategy:** Offer a "Realtor/Inspector Rush Service" (priority scheduling for pending sales).
    - **Asset:** Create a dedicated PDF one-pager they can give to clients.
- **Waterproofers (who don't do cracks):**
    - **Strategy:** Establish a two-way referral system. They do the drainage; we do the structural cracks.
- **Real Estate Agencies:**
    - **Strategy:** Offer "Lunch & Learn" sessions to teach agents how to spot foundation issues.

## 3. Review Generation & Reputation Management

**Goal:** Consistent flow of 4-5 star reviews to signal active business operations.

- **The "Strike While Hot" Protocol:**
    - Request review **immediately** upon job completion (while technician is on-site or same-day text).
    - Use a QR code card that goes directly to the Google Review form.
- **Response Strategy:**
    - **Speed:** Reply to all reviews within 48 hours.
    - **Keywords:** Use service keywords in replies (e.g., "Glad we could fix that *foundation crack* in *West Hartford* for you!").
    - **Negatives:** Always reply professionally, offering to take it offline. Never argue publicly.

## 4. Local Service Ads (LSA) & Google Guarantee

**Goal:** Appear at the very top of search results, above traditional ads and maps.

- **Google Guarantee Badge:**
    - **Requirement:** Background checks, insurance verification.
    - **Status:** **CRITICAL**. If you don't have this, you lose the top 3 spots automatically in 2026.
    - **Action:** Ensure LSA profile is active and budget is managed weekly. Even a small budget keeps the badge active.

## 5. Offline to Online (O2O)

**Goal:** Use physical presence to drive digital signals.

- **Yard Signs:**
    - **Strategy:** "Another Foundation Saved by Attack A Crack".
    - **QR Code:** Add a QR code to the sign linking to a "Check Your Foundation" checklist page.
- **Truck Wraps:**
    - **Audit:** Ensure URL and Phone are massive and high-contrast.
- **Direct Mail:**
    - **Targeting:** New homeowners (0-6 months post-purchase) in older neighborhoods.
    - **Offer:** "Free Foundation Health Check" for new owners.
