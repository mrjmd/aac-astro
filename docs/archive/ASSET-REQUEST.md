## Video Phase 0: Three Quick Clips

These don't need to be produced — just raw phone clips. 10-15 seconds each, horizontal orientation.

1. **Diamond saw cutting the groove** — Close-up of the saw blade cutting along a crack
2. **Resin injection** — Close-up of the injection gun connected to a port, resin flowing
3. **Finished repair** — Slow pan along a completed, sealed crack (ports trimmed, clean wall)

These clips can go on YouTube, be embedded on service pages, and posted to GBP. One job site visit = all three clips in under 2 minutes.

## Quick Reference: Image Dimensions

| Use | Minimum Size | Ideal Size | Format |
|-----|-------------|-----------|--------|
| Hero images | 800x600 | 1200x800 | `.webp` preferred, `.jpg` fine |
| Before/After pairs | 800x600 | 800x600 | `.webp` or `.jpg` |
| Blog post images | 800x450 | 1200x630 | `.webp` or `.jpg` |
| OG/social share | 1200x630 | 1200x630 | `.jpg` (most compatible) |

**Tip:** Phone photos are perfect. Just make sure they're well-lit and in focus. Horizontal (landscape) is preferred for most uses.
