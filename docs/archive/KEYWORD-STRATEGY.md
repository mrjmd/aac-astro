## Part 7: Technical SEO Checklist

### Schema Markup Priorities

- [x] LocalBusiness schema on all pages (implemented)
- [x] FAQ schema on service and city pages (implemented)
- [x] AggregateRating schema (implemented)
- [ ] HowTo schema on service pages (add to wall-crack-repair, etc.)
- [ ] Article schema on blog posts
- [x] BreadcrumbList schema (implemented)

### On-Page Optimization Checklist

For each priority keyword:

- [ ] Keyword in H1 (exactly or close variant)
- [ ] Keyword in meta title (front-loaded)
- [ ] Keyword in meta description (with call-to-action)
- [ ] Keyword in first 100 words of content
- [ ] Keyword in at least one H2
- [ ] Internal links from relevant pages using keyword anchor text
- [ ] External links to authoritative sources (optional)
- [ ] Image alt text includes keyword variant
- [ ] URL slug includes primary keyword

### Internal Linking Strategy

| From Page Type | To Page Type | Anchor Text Pattern |
|----------------|--------------|---------------------|
| City pages | Service pages | "[service name]" or "[service] in [city]" |
| Service pages | City pages | "serving [city]" or "[city] [service]" |
| Blog posts | Service pages | "[keyword] services" |
| Blog posts | City pages | "foundation repair in [city]" |
| Service pages | Blog posts | "learn more about [topic]" |
