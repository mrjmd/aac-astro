## Background

AAC currently has 30 MA city pages covering Metro Boston, North Shore, South Shore, Metrowest, Greater Fall River, and Central Mass. This plan adds 41 cities in 5 prioritized phases based on business ROI:

1. **CityLocal 101 Gap** — Matt pays for listings with no AAC pages (cancel CityLocal after)
2. **Index Page Gaps** — Cities already in `maRegions` but rendering as plain text (no pages)
3. **Zone 1 Fortress** — South Shore core market around Quincy HQ
4. **Cape Cod** — New region Matt specifically requested; Pioneer covers it, AAC doesn't
5. **Competitor-Targeted** — Cities where Pioneer/Groundworks have pages but AAC doesn't

- Emphasize sandy glacial soils, salt air corrosion, high water tables
- Address seasonal/vacation home concerns (winterization, freeze-thaw)
- Hurricane and nor'easter foundation stress
- Proximity to Joint Base Cape Cod (Bourne) for military housing
- Each city must have genuinely distinct content — don't just repeat "Cape Cod sandy soils"

**Standard Cities (all others):**
- Use `brockton.md` as template
- City character paragraph + geology/soil paragraph + AAC pitch paragraph
- 3 FAQs: cost, local geology, area-specific concern

**All Cities:**
- Every metaDescription must be unique (120-160 chars, no duplicates)
- Reference real landmarks, rivers, neighborhoods — no generic content
- Smaller towns need extra differentiation effort (specific rivers, industries, housing eras)
- The ServiceAreaMap component auto-generates pins from coordinates — no map changes needed
- No 301 redirects needed (no pages being removed)
