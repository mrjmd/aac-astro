## 1. Strategy Overview: The "Expert Jester" on Camera

In 2026, Google rewards **Experience**. Seeing Luc and Matt in their gear, talking about foundation science, proves to Google (and homeowners) that this isn't a lead-gen shell site—it's a real business with 30+ years of expertise.

### Core Objectives:
1.  **Lower the Shield:** Homeowners are nervous about "contractors." Seeing your faces and hearing your voice lowers their defensive shield.

3.  **Capture AI Snippets:** Google's AI Overviews "watch" video transcripts. Answering a specific question on camera is the fastest way to win the "How to fix a foundation crack" snippet.

### C. Service Page: The Leaky Bulkhead
**Goal:** Address the "Cold Joint" problem.

> "In New England, the bulkhead door is the #1 place basements leak. Why? Because of the 'cold joint'—that’s the spot where your bulkhead stairs meet your foundation wall. They aren't poured as one piece, so water finds the gap. 
> 
> Most people try to fix this with a rubber gasket or some hardware store sealant. It lasts about one season. We use high-pressure polyurethane injection to create a permanent, flexible seal inside that joint. It’s the difference between a band-aid and a permanent solution. Let’s keep that cellar dry."

## 5. Technical Implementation (The SEO "Magic")

Recording the video is only half the battle. We need Google to "read" it.

1.  **Hosting:** Upload to **YouTube**. (Second largest search engine).
2.  **Embed:** Use the YouTube embed on the relevant page.
3.  **VideoObject Schema:** We will add JSON-LD schema to the page that includes:
    *   `name`: "How to Repair a Foundation Crack - Attack A Crack"
    *   `description`: "Luc explains the difference between epoxy and polyurethane..."
    *   `contentUrl`: The YouTube link.
    *   `transcript`: A full text version (Google loves this for AI snippets).
4.  **Thumbnail:** We will use a custom thumbnail with "Expert Jester" style text (e.g., "Don't Fall for the Flex Seal Lie").
