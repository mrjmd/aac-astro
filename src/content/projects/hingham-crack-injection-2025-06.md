A property in Hingham, MA, was found to have two distinct ‘shortie’ foundation cracks visible in the basement wall. These cracks, despite their smaller size, presented a clear risk for water intrusion and potential future structural concerns. Our skilled technicians performed a professional foundation crack injection, expertly sealing both cracks with specialized materials, which fully restored the wall's integrity and eliminated the risk of water penetration, ensuring a dry and stable foundation.
