A Middleborough, MA basement had multiple wall cracks allowing water intrusion, including a challenging vertical crack beneath a window that showed signs of previous, unsuccessful sealing attempts. Our crew thoroughly prepared the damaged concrete, removing deteriorated material before applying high-performance waterproof sealants to each of the four identified cracks. We also sealed the bulkhead area, eliminating all sources of water penetration and providing a durable, long-lasting solution.
