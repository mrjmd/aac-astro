A Middleborough, MA basement required bulkhead repair due to multiple cracks, including a challenging vertical crack beneath a window that exhibited signs of previous, unsuccessful sealing attempts. Our crew thoroughly prepared the damaged concrete, removing deteriorated material before applying high-performance waterproof sealants to each of the four identified cracks. The meticulous repair work successfully fortified the bulkhead, eliminating all sources of water penetration and providing a durable, long-lasting solution.
