An older concrete foundation, dating back to the 1930s in Quincy, MA, was experiencing active water leaks. A thorough inspection revealed a vertical crack near the top of the wall, a horizontal crack extending left, and a pinhole leak further down, all allowing water intrusion. Our specialized team addressed these specific vulnerabilities by expertly applying foundation crack injection, which effectively and permanently sealed all identified leaks, restoring the integrity of the concrete wall and ensuring a dry basement.
