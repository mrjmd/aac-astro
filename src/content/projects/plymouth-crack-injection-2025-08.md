In Plymouth, MA, a property required foundation repair due to minor cracks and gaps surrounding its main sewer and gas line penetrations. These breaches presented a clear pathway for potential water intrusion into the basement. Our team expertly performed targeted foundation crack injections using a high-quality polyurethane sealant, effectively filling and sealing these critical entry points. This process successfully waterproofed the foundation, preventing future moisture issues and safeguarding the home's structural integrity.
