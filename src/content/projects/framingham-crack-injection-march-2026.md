An 8-foot vertical crack in a poured concrete foundation was causing basement flooding during every rainstorm. Our team injected the crack with polyurethane resin at high pressure, sealing it from the interior surface through to the exterior soil. The repair was completed in under two hours with a lifetime guarantee.
