A Norwell, MA foundation was suffering from significant water intrusion, visible as active leaks and extensive staining from two distinct wall cracks and five tie rod penetrations. One of the cracks, located at a corner, was notably thin, presenting a delicate challenge. Our foundation specialists meticulously performed a comprehensive crack injection service, utilizing high-pressure polyurethane resin. This specialized material was expertly injected into both the visible cracks and all five tie rod holes, ensuring complete saturation and permanent sealing of the breaches. As a result, the foundation was successfully waterproofed, eliminating all leaks and preventing future water damage.
