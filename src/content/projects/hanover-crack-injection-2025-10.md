A basement in Hanover, MA experienced significant water intrusion from two active foundation cracks, with notable leaking concentrated around a window well. Foundation repair specialists addressed these issues by expertly injecting a high-grade polyurethane sealant into both vertical cracks. This effective repair successfully stopped all water leaks, restoring the integrity of the foundation and ensuring a dry, protected basement environment.
