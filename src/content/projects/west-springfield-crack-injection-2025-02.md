A property in West Springfield, MA, was experiencing issues with foundation cracks in its bulkhead and a noticeable gap in the garage floor. To address these problems, a specialized foundation crack injection process was performed on the bulkhead to seal all visible fissures. Additionally, the garage floor gap was meticulously sealed using a durable Sikaflex sealant, resulting in a completely waterproofed and secure foundation for the home.
