In Worcester, MA, a property owner was experiencing water intrusion from an actively leaking foundation crack, alongside an older, deteriorating repair from a different company. Our team performed a professional crack injection on the active leak, creating a durable, watertight seal. The previous repair was meticulously cleaned and re-sealed with high-quality Sikaflex, fully restoring the foundation's integrity and ensuring a permanently dry basement environment.
