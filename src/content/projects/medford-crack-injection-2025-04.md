A property located in Medford, MA, presented with two clear vertical cracks in its concrete foundation wall. These cracks posed a risk for water intrusion and potential long-term structural issues. Our expert team promptly addressed these concerns by performing a foundation crack injection service. Utilizing high-pressure polyurethane, both cracks were meticulously injected and sealed, creating a durable and watertight barrier. This crucial repair successfully stabilized the foundation, preventing future water seepage and reinforcing the structural integrity of the home.
