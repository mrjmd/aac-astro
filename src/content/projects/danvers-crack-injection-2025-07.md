A Danvers, MA property was experiencing persistent water intrusion through a vertical foundation crack, made worse by a leaking pipe penetration in the concrete wall. Evidence of previous, ineffective repairs and water staining indicated a long-standing moisture problem. Our team efficiently addressed the issue by expertly injecting the full length of the foundation crack with a durable polyurethane sealant, then thoroughly prepping and sealing the area around the pipe penetration with a robust hydraulic cement patch, successfully eliminating water leakage and ensuring the basement remains dry and secure.
