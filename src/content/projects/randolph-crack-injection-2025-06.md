A property in Randolph, MA, required foundation repair due to water seeping through a visible crack and a couple of tie rod holes in the basement wall. Our specialists addressed the issue by performing a targeted foundation crack injection service. High-pressure polyurethane resin was injected into the crack and around the tie rod penetrations, effectively creating a durable, watertight seal and ensuring the dryness and structural integrity of the basement.
