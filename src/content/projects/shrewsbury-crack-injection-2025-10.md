A home in Shrewsbury, MA, required extensive foundation crack repair across multiple areas. Identified issues included a significant crack in the garage that needed structural stapling and injection, another garage crack best sealed from the exterior, a vertical cold joint where concrete met cinder block, and two distinct window cracks. Our team thoroughly addressed these vulnerabilities with expert crack injection techniques using appropriate materials, alongside structural reinforcement for the more critical garage crack. These precise repairs successfully sealed all openings, preventing future water intrusion and reinforcing the foundation's structural integrity.
