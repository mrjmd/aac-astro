A property in Braintree, MA, experienced a noticeable zig-zagging crack in its concrete foundation wall, leading to unsightly moisture stains and potential water intrusion. To resolve this, specialized foundation crack injection techniques were employed, using a high-pressure polyurethane resin to completely fill and seal the crack from the inside out. This repair successfully waterproofed the foundation, securing the basement against future leaks and reinforcing the structural integrity of the wall.
