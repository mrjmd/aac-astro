A Marshfield, MA, property presented with three foundation cracks in its basement. One particular crack was wider than the others and exhibited noticeable displacement, suggesting a more significant structural concern. Our technicians successfully addressed all three cracks using specialized injection techniques. The wider, displaced crack received an epoxy injection for enhanced structural bonding, while the others were sealed, ensuring the entire foundation is now watertight and structurally stable.
