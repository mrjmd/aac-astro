A property in Marshfield, MA, presented with two concerning foundation cracks in its basement: a prominent full-length crack and a wider crack located near a window opening. These issues indicated potential water intrusion points and compromised structural integrity. The foundation repair team performed a specialized crack injection service, utilizing high-pressure polyurethane to permanently seal both fissures from the interior. This professional intervention effectively waterproofed the foundation and restored its stability.
