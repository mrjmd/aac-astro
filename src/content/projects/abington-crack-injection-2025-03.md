In Abington, MA, a significant settling crack in a garage foundation was allowing water to seep into the property. To address this issue, a comprehensive foundation crack injection was performed, effectively sealing the leak and preventing further water intrusion. Additionally, a larger crack discovered in the adjacent deck foundation was sealed with hydraulic cement, ensuring the long-term stability and integrity of both structures.
