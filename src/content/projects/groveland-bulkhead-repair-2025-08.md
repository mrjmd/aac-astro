A property in Groveland, MA was experiencing persistent water intrusion into its basement due to a significant leak around the bulkhead entry. The compromised seal allowed moisture to penetrate the foundation, creating a damp and potentially damaging environment. Our expert team performed a comprehensive bulkhead repair, meticulously addressing the failed seals and reinforcing the structure. Specialized techniques and high-quality, durable materials were utilized to create a robust and watertight barrier, completely eliminating the leaks and restoring the integrity of the bulkhead.
