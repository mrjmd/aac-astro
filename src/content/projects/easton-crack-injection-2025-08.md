This Easton, MA, property presented with several foundation vulnerabilities, including a crack extending from a basement window, three leaking tie rod holes, another full-length basement crack, and three smaller cracks in the garage. To address these issues, a comprehensive foundation crack injection service was performed. All identified cracks and tie rod penetrations were sealed, including exterior hairline cracks where applicable, successfully preventing further water intrusion and stabilizing the foundation.
