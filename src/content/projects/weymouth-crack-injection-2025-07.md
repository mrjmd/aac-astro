A property in Weymouth, MA experienced water intrusion from a vertical crack directly beneath a basement window, causing significant dampness. Our expert technicians performed a foundation crack injection, utilizing a high-grade polyurethane material to fully seal the actively leaking crack, effectively addressing the harder-to-reach exterior section behind the window well. This successful injection permanently stopped all water leaks, restoring the foundation's integrity and providing a dry basement.
