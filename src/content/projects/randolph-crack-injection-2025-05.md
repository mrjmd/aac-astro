In Randolph, MA, a homeowner finishing their basement discovered a new leak originating from a full-length foundation crack hidden behind recently installed drywall. Our team strategically cut open the drywall to access the problematic crack. A high-pressure crack injection was then performed, successfully sealing the entire length of the fissure and preventing future water intrusion, allowing the homeowner to complete their basement project with confidence.
