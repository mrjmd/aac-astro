Homeowners in Sandwich, MA, discovered a persistent water leak emanating from a vertical crack in their basement foundation wall, located directly beneath a window. This issue was addressed with a professional foundation crack injection service. Our experienced technicians meticulously prepared the crack, then utilized a high-pressure polyurethane injection to permanently seal the fissure, preventing any further water intrusion. The repair successfully eliminated the leak, ensuring the basement remained dry and restoring the integrity of the home's foundation.
