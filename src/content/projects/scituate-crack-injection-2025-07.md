A Scituate, MA home experienced significant water intrusion from multiple points in its foundation. The project involved a comprehensive repair strategy, sealing two actively leaking cracks with polyurethane, addressing a larger structural crack with epoxy injection, and waterproofing a leaking conduit line. Additionally, a hairline crack received a surface patch, and two potential window cracks were proactively sealed for complete protection. This extensive foundation crack injection restored the basement to a dry and secure condition, ensuring long-term integrity against future water damage.
