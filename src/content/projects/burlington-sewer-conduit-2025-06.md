A Burlington, MA property suffered water intrusion through a foundation crack that was directly connected to a sewer line inlet. This allowed moisture to penetrate the basement wall and potentially compromise the utility line connection. Our team performed a comprehensive repair by meticulously sealing the crack, effectively stopping the leak, ensuring a dry foundation, and safeguarding the sewer line from future issues.
