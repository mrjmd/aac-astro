A homeowner in Billerica, MA, discovered a significant vertical crack in their foundation wall that extended from the interior to the exterior, posing a risk for water penetration and structural compromise. The foundation repair team effectively addressed this issue by performing a professional crack injection. A specialized polyurethane material was expertly injected, permanently sealing the defect, preventing future water intrusion, and restoring the integrity of the foundation.
