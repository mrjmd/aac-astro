A concrete foundation in Quincy, MA, displayed a prominent vertical crack and an exposed tie rod, indicating potential water intrusion and structural weakness. Our team addressed the issue by performing a targeted foundation crack injection, which involved sealing the crack and the associated tie rod from within. This comprehensive repair effectively waterproofed the wall and restored its structural integrity, preventing further moisture problems and ensuring lasting durability for the home.
