A critical foundation crack was identified in a Hingham, MA property, compromising structural integrity and posing a significant risk for water intrusion. To resolve this, specialized foundation crack injection techniques were expertly applied. Technicians prepared the area and then injected a high-pressure, flexible sealant deep into the void, creating a complete and watertight fill. This process successfully sealed the foundation, providing a durable barrier against moisture and fully restoring its structural stability, ensuring the property's long-term protection.
