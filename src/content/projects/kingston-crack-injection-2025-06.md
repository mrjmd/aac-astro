A Kingston, MA foundation required comprehensive waterproofing due to two significant vertical cracks, a horizontal crack near a bulkhead door, and 18 leaking tie rod holes. Our experienced team meticulously injected each crack and every tie rod hole with specialized sealant. This thorough application successfully eliminated water intrusion, restoring the foundation's integrity and ensuring a dry, protected basement for the property.
