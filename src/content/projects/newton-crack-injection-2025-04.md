A homeowner in Newton, MA, contacted our team regarding a prominent vertical crack in their basement foundation wall, extending from under a window and raising concerns about potential water intrusion. Our technicians promptly assessed the issue and performed a professional foundation crack injection, meticulously cleaning the crack, installing injection ports, and pressure-injecting a specialized, expanding polyurethane material. This process successfully sealed the crack from the inside out, creating a permanent, watertight barrier that restored the foundation's stability and ensured a dry, secure basement.
