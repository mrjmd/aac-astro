A vertical foundation crack causing moisture issues was identified in a Belchertown, MA, basement. The crack's proximity to sensitive wall-mounted solar inverters presented a unique challenge, requiring a precise repair strategy. Our team meticulously performed a polyurethane injection to seal the crack, ensuring no disruption to the electrical equipment while effectively waterproofing the foundation.
