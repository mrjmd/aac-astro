A concrete foundation wall at a Marshfield, MA property exhibited a vertical crack that allowed water intrusion, exacerbated by three compromised tie rod penetrations. Our foundation repair team deployed a specialized crack injection service to permanently resolve the issue. The crack and all three tie rod locations were meticulously prepared and injected with high-grade material, fully sealing the foundation and restoring its waterproof integrity.
