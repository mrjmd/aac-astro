A concrete foundation wall in a Plymouth, MA home displayed a notable vertical crack, which presented a risk of water intrusion and compromised structural stability. Our skilled technicians promptly performed a foundation crack injection, meticulously preparing the area and injecting a specialized polymer sealant deep into the fissure. This process effectively sealed the crack from the inside out, permanently preventing leaks and restoring the wall's strength and durability.
