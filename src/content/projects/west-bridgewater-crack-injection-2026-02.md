A property in West Bridgewater, MA was experiencing water intrusion due to three visible foundation cracks that were concealed behind interior drywall. To properly address the issue, sections of drywall were carefully cut out to expose the full extent of each crack. Our team then performed a specialized foundation crack injection, effectively sealing all three cracks and restoring the structural integrity of the foundation, ensuring a dry and stable basement.
