Four small concrete stairways in Boston had deteriorated significantly, showing cracks and missing chunks that compromised safety and detracted from the property's curb appeal. Our team expertly addressed this by resurfacing the stairs using specialized concrete repair techniques, which involved preparing the existing surfaces and applying a durable, high-quality overlay. This project successfully transformed the stairs, providing a smooth, uniform finish that enhanced both their structural integrity and aesthetic appeal for residents.
