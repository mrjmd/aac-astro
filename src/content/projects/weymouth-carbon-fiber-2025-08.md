A cinder block foundation wall at a Weymouth, MA property exhibited significant cracking, compromising its structural integrity. To resolve this, our team installed eight high-strength carbon fiber staples across the affected areas. This specialized reinforcement effectively stabilized the wall, preventing further movement and ensuring the long-term structural stability of the foundation.
