Two foundation cracks, including one near a basement window in East Bridgewater, MA, were identified as potential sources of water intrusion. The proactive homeowner had removed the drywall, ensuring direct access to the foundation for repair. Our skilled team utilized high-pressure polyurethane injection to thoroughly fill and seal both cracks, creating a flexible and waterproof barrier that penetrates the entire wall thickness. This specialized repair successfully eliminated pathways for water, restoring the foundation's integrity and ensuring a permanently dry basement for the property owner.
