In a home located in Milton, MA, a vertical crack had formed in the concrete foundation wall, extending from beneath a basement window. This crack was a direct source of significant water intrusion, resulting in noticeable pooling on the basement floor. Our skilled team promptly addressed the issue by performing a professional foundation crack injection. Utilizing a specialized polyurethane material, the crack was thoroughly sealed, effectively stopping all water leakage and restoring the foundation's integrity and watertight condition.
