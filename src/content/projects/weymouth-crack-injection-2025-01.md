A property in Weymouth, MA, was experiencing issues with a prominent settling crack in its concrete foundation wall. This crack allowed persistent moisture intrusion, evidenced by significant efflorescence and water staining, which compromised the basement's dryness. Our team of foundation repair specialists assessed the damage and performed a targeted crack injection using a high-quality, expanding polyurethane resin. This effectively sealed the entire depth and width of the crack, creating a durable, watertight barrier that stopped further leaks and restored the foundation's integrity.
