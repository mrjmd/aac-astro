A homeowner in Norwell, MA, experienced water intrusion through a vertical crack in their concrete bulkhead foundation wall, evident by water staining. Our team diagnosed the issue and performed a targeted foundation crack injection using high-pressure polyurethane. This process permanently sealed the crack from within, completely stopping the water leaks and restoring the integrity of the home's foundation, ensuring a dry and secure basement.
