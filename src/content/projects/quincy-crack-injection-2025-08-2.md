A property in Quincy, MA, was experiencing significant water intrusion through multiple foundation cracks, including two long and two short floor cracks, and a vertical wall crack. Our expert team performed comprehensive foundation crack injection on all these entry points. Additionally, a horizontal wall crack was sealed with Sika, completely waterproofing the area and restoring a dry, secure environment.
