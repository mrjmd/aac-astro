A property in Canton, MA, required foundation crack repair for two distinct issues, including a window crack that had previously undergone an ineffective and unsightly repair attempt. The existing patch was peeling and visually unappealing, failing to provide a durable seal against water intrusion. The repair team meticulously removed the failed material, cleaned the foundation surface, and then professionally injected both cracks using a high-pressure polyurethane solution. This process successfully waterproofed the foundation, permanently sealing the cracks and leaving the wall with a clean, neat, and durable finish.
