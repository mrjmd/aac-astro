A property in Hanover, MA, was experiencing significant water intrusion through a hairline foundation crack. Despite a previous attempt at sealing, water continued to leak, suggesting the issue originated from the lower wall and potentially beneath the slab. Our team implemented a specialized foundation crack injection, carefully targeting the full length of the defect, with particular attention to the challenging bottom section. This comprehensive repair successfully halted all water infiltration, ensuring a dry and stable basement.
