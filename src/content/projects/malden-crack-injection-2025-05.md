A rock foundation wall in a Malden, MA property was experiencing persistent water intrusion, visible as puddling on the adjacent floor. Our team identified the latest leak source within the natural rock structure and applied specialized foundation crack injection. This process successfully sealed the water entry points, effectively resolving the recurring leak and ensuring a dry, protected basement environment.
