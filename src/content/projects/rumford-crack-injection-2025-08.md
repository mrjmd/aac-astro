A finished basement in Rumford, RI experienced significant water intrusion and mold growth due to a vertical foundation crack, with nearby tie rods also contributing to the issue. Our team performed a specialized polyurethane crack injection, meticulously sealing the entire vertical crack and addressing the problematic tie rod penetrations. This comprehensive repair successfully eliminated all water seepage, transforming the previously damp area into a dry and healthy basement space.
