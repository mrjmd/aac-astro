A prominent vertical crack was observed in the foundation slab of a Milton, MA property, raising concerns about structural integrity and potential water intrusion. To effectively address this issue, technicians performed a comprehensive foundation crack injection to seal the fissure. Additionally, two carbon fiber staples were expertly installed across the crack, providing essential reinforcement and ensuring long-term stability for the foundation.
