A property in Randolph, MA exhibited a significant vertical crack in its concrete foundation wall, a common issue that can compromise structural integrity and allow for water penetration into the basement. To address this concern, our team implemented a professional foundation crack injection service. A high-grade, expanding polyurethane material was precisely injected into the crack, filling the void completely from the interior to the exterior. This process successfully sealed the foundation, preventing future water seepage and reinforcing the wall for lasting stability.
