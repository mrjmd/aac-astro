A property in South Yarmouth, MA, required concrete repair for two cracks in its basement foundation wall. One crack showed signs of potential movement, necessitating a more robust repair solution. Our team professionally sealed both cracks; the more significant one was reinforced with five structural staples to provide enhanced stability and prevent future expansion. This comprehensive intervention successfully restored the wall's integrity, ensuring a secure and stable basement for the property.
