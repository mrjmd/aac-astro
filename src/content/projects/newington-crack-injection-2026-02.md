A foundation in Newington, CT, was experiencing water intrusion at the site of an old, infilled window well. The original concrete and asphalt infill had failed, allowing moisture to penetrate the basement wall. Our team performed a comprehensive foundation crack injection from the interior, while also meticulously sealing the exterior window well area with new concrete and asphalt. This dual-approach successfully stopped the leaks, resulting in a permanently waterproofed foundation and a dry, secure basement.
