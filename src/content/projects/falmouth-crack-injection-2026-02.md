A Falmouth, MA property was experiencing persistent water seepage through a foundation crack located adjacent to their main water line entry point. This recurring issue was causing dampness and concern in the basement area. Our foundation repair specialists carefully cleaned and prepared the affected section of the wall. A specialized polyurethane crack injection material was then meticulously applied, filling the entire crack from the inside out under high pressure. This precise application effectively sealed the penetration, immediately stopping all water intrusion and restoring the foundation's integrity at this critical juncture.
