A property in Hanover, MA experienced persistent water intrusion due to a leaking hairline foundation crack. Despite a prior attempt to seal the crack, water continued to penetrate the basement, visible on the floor and wall. Our team utilized a specialized foundation crack injection process, meticulously applying a sealing solution to thoroughly fill the crack, with particular focus on the lower section to counteract water migration. This expert intervention successfully eliminated all leakage, securing the foundation's integrity and ensuring a dry, protected basement space.
