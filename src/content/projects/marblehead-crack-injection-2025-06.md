A property in Marblehead, MA, suffered from water intrusion due to a significant vertical crack in its basement foundation wall, directly below a shortie window. This active leak was evidenced by persistent efflorescence and rust staining. Our team expertly performed a foundation crack injection, permanently sealing the breach and stopping all water entry, successfully restoring a dry and protected basement.
