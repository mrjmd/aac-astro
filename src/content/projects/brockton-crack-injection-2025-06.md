A Brockton, MA foundation exhibited several issues, including two significant cracks – one at a well line and another at a corner cold joint – plus a minor bulkhead crack, and a large spalled area outside the garage. The team performed expert crack injections for the major fissures, sealed the small crack with Sikaflex, and professionally resurfaced the entire spalled concrete section. This comprehensive repair restored the foundation's structural integrity and improved its exterior appearance.
