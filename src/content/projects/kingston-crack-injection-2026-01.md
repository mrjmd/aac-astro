A property in Kingston, MA presented with a significant, full-length vertical crack in its concrete foundation wall, indicating a potential pathway for water intrusion and structural weakness. Our team expertly addressed this issue by performing a polyurethane crack injection. The high-grade, expanding material was injected directly into the crack, creating a durable and watertight seal that effectively prevented future leaks and restored the foundation's long-term integrity.
