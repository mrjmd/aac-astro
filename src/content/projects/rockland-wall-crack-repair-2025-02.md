Newly constructed foundation walls at two development homes in Rockland, MA, presented several vertical cracks. These defects were identified as stress-induced, likely stemming from site contractor work during garage infills. Our team performed specialized wall crack repair services, carefully sealing and stabilizing the affected areas to restore the structural integrity of both foundations. The successful repairs ensured the homes were secure and ready for subsequent construction phases.
