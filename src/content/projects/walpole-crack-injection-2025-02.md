A property in Walpole, MA was experiencing water intrusion through a clearly visible and straightforward leaking crack in its concrete foundation. The area around the crack, which had been somewhat obstructed, was cleared to facilitate optimal access for the repair. Our skilled technicians performed a professional foundation crack injection, effectively sealing the breach and permanently stopping the water leak. This precise repair reinforced the foundation, restoring its integrity and preventing any future moisture issues.
