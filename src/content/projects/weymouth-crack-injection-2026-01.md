A Weymouth, MA property required attention for several foundation issues, including a prominent crack near a window exhibiting clear signs of water leakage. Our expert technicians performed a comprehensive crack injection to permanently seal this primary water entry point. Furthermore, three additional non-leaking cracks were surface-sealed, and a small area of exterior concrete chipping was resurfaced, ensuring the foundation's stability and protecting the home from future water damage.
