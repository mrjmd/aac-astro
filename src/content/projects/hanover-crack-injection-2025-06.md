A property in Hanover, MA presented with several foundation cracks requiring attention. A prominent full-length vertical crack and a shorter, additional crack were professionally injected to ensure a watertight and stable repair. Smaller cosmetic cracks were surface-sealed with Sika, effectively waterproofing the entire foundation and preventing future water intrusion.
