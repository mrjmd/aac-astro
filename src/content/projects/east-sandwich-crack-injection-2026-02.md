A property in East Sandwich, MA, was experiencing water intrusion and potential structural concerns due to a significant vertical foundation crack. Our expert team performed a comprehensive foundation crack injection, using high-pressure polyurethane to create a permanent, watertight seal and strategically installing five carbon fiber staples to reinforce the wall and prevent future movement. This dual-action repair successfully halted all water leakage, restored the foundation's structural integrity, and provided lasting stability for the property.
