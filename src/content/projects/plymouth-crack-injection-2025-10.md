A concrete foundation in Plymouth, MA, was experiencing water intrusion through a short, hairline crack. This issue caused persistent dampness and concern for the structural integrity of the basement. Our expert team performed a targeted foundation crack injection, utilizing a specialized polyurethane material to completely fill and seal the problematic crack. This effective repair successfully halted the water entry, providing a long-lasting, watertight solution and restoring the dryness and safety of the foundation.
