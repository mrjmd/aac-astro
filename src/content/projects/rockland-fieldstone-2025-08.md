This Rockland, MA crawl space was plagued by severe water intrusion originating from its fieldstone foundation, causing significant pooling along the perimeter. To rectify this, a specialized interior perimeter drainage system was installed, expertly diverting incoming water away. The result was a completely dry and stabilized crawl space, protecting the home from future moisture damage.
