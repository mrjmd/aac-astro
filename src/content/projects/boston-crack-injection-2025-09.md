A Boston property presented with a severe, full-height crack in its foundation wall, causing significant water intrusion and dampness. Our expert team performed a foundation crack injection, utilizing a high-grade polyurethane sealant to thoroughly fill the extensive fissure. This process successfully sealed the entire crack, permanently stopping leaks and restoring the structural integrity of the foundation.
