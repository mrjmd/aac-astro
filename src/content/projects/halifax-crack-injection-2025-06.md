A homeowner in Halifax, MA, contacted our team about a persistent vertical crack in their basement foundation wall that was allowing water to seep into the living space. The crack, which showed signs of efflorescence and previous repair attempts, needed a reliable, long-term solution to prevent further moisture issues. Our specialists performed a professional foundation crack injection, expertly sealing the entire length of the fissure with a durable, watertight material. This repair successfully stopped all water intrusion, restored the structural integrity of the foundation, and provided the homeowner with a dry, secure basement.
