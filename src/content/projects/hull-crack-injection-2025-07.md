A historic Hull, MA home from 1900 was experiencing an active water leak through a crack in its poured concrete foundation. Our team verified the foundation type and then performed a specialized foundation crack injection. This process effectively sealed the crack, permanently stopping the water intrusion and restoring the basement's dry and stable condition.
