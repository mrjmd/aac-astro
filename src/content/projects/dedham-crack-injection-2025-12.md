A fieldstone foundation in Dedham, MA, suffered from persistent water intrusion through multiple cracks and deteriorating mortar joints, particularly visible in four distinct areas. To resolve this, specialized foundation crack injection techniques were utilized. A high-quality polyurethane resin was injected into the compromised sections, effectively sealing the leaks and restoring the foundation's integrity, ensuring a dry basement.
