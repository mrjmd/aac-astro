## Walkway & Stairway Concrete Repair in Massachusetts

Your walkway and front steps are the first things guests see when they visit your home. When they're cracked, stained, or crumbling, they create trip hazards and drag down your curb appeal. Concrete walkway and stairway repair fills cracks with epoxy resin or polymer-modified materials and gives you a fresh, durable surface — without the cost and disruption of full replacement.

**Concrete vs. cement:** You might search for "cement walkway repair" or "cement step repair" — cement is actually just one ingredient in concrete (like flour in bread). Your walkway and stairs are concrete, and our repair system bonds directly to it.

### The Problem with Old Walkways and Stairs

Massachusetts walkways and stairs endure brutal conditions:
- **Freeze-thaw cycles** that crack concrete from within
- **De-icing salts** that pit and erode surfaces
- **Tree roots** pushing up walkway sections
- **Constant foot traffic** wearing down stair edges
- **Age and weathering** that dull once-attractive concrete
- **Oil and rust stains** that seem impossible to remove

Over time, these factors turn attractive walkways into eyesores — and worn stairs into safety hazards.

### Why Repair Works

Our polymer-modified overlay repairs existing damage and creates a new wearing surface that:
- **Bonds permanently** to existing concrete
- **Bridges minor cracks** to prevent water infiltration
- **Resists freeze-thaw** better than original concrete
- **Costs 30-50% less** than full replacement
- **Finishes in a day** instead of weeks for replacement

### Walkway Repair

Walkway resurfacing addresses surface cracks, spalling, staining, and rough texture. We clean and profile the existing surface, repair all cracks, and apply a polymer-modified overlay that creates a fresh wearing surface. Basic color matching is available to complement your home's exterior.

**Common walkway problems we fix:**
- Surface cracks and crazing
- Spalling and flaking
- Stains and discoloration
- Rough, uneven texture
- Trip hazards from settled sections

### Stairway Repair

Concrete stairs demand extra attention — especially around safety. Every stair repair includes non-slip additives in the finish, with special attention to nosing (stair edges) where slips most often occur.

**Common stair problems we fix:**
- Surface spalling (flaking, peeling concrete)
- Crack networks and individual cracks
- Exposed aggregate from surface erosion
- Salt damage and pitting
- Uneven or settling steps
- Worn, unsafe appearance

**Indoor stairs too:** While most of our stair work is exterior, we also repair basement stairs, garage entry stairs, and commercial interior stairs.

### Our Repair Process

**Step 1: Evaluation**
We assess your walkway or stairs — most assessments can be done from photos you text us. We'll tell you honestly if repair is the right approach or if replacement makes more sense.

**Step 2: Preparation**
Thorough cleaning, crack repair, and surface profiling prepare the concrete for the overlay.

**Step 3: Overlay Application**
Multiple coats of polymer-modified overlay are applied, each carefully troweled to specification.

**Step 4: Texture and Seal**
We apply the desired texture — including non-slip finish on all stair surfaces — and an optional high-quality sealant topcoat for added protection.

### Timeline

Most walkways and stairways are completed in a single day. Larger or more complex projects may take two days.

**Cure Time:**
- Foot traffic: 24 hours
- Full cure: 2-3 days

### When to Consider Replacement Instead

We believe in honest recommendations. Repair isn't always the answer:
- **Severely heaved or settled sections** — the base needs correction before any overlay
- **Structurally compromised stairs** — if the slab has shifted or broken away from the foundation
- **Major structural damage** — when more than 50% of the surface is severely damaged
- **Tree root damage** — address the cause before resurfacing

We'll give you an honest assessment during your free consultation.
