## Foundation Repair Services in Exeter, New Hampshire

Exeter is one of New Hampshire's most historic towns, a community whose roots stretch back to 1638. As the state's original capital, Exeter has an extraordinary collection of historic homes alongside more modern residential development. This blend of old and new creates diverse foundation repair needs. Downtown Exeter and the surrounding neighborhoods feature homes with fieldstone and brick foundations that have endured centuries of New England weather, while areas along the Epping Road corridor and Brentwood Road have newer homes on poured concrete foundations.

Water is a constant concern for Exeter homeowners. The town sits at the head of the Squamscott River, a tidal river that influences groundwater levels well beyond the immediate riverbanks. During spring snowmelt and king tides, water tables rise substantially, pushing moisture against basement walls throughout the low-lying areas of town. Even on higher ground near Jady Hill, the combination of New Hampshire's 4-5 foot frost line and seasonal moisture fluctuations drives crack formation in foundations of all types and ages.
