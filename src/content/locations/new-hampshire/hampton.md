Salt air, storm surge, high water tables, and sandy soils all conspire to stress foundation walls in ways that require specialized repair knowledge.

The sandy coastal soils beneath Hampton provide good drainage under normal conditions but can shift during storms and heavy weather, placing lateral loads on basement walls that open cracks in poured concrete and block foundations.
