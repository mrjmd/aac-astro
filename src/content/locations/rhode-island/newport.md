## Foundation Repair Services in Newport, Rhode Island

Newport is renowned for its extraordinary concentration of colonial, Gilded Age, and historic architecture, and the foundations beneath these treasured buildings face unique pressures found nowhere else in Rhode Island. Surrounded by water on three sides, Newport's homes endure constant salt air exposure, elevated coastal humidity, and some of the highest water tables in the state. Attack A Crack brings the specialized skills needed to repair foundations in this demanding marine environment.

The city's housing spans centuries of construction techniques. Colonial-era homes on The Point and Historic Hill sit on fieldstone foundations laid in the 1700s and 1800s, while later Victorian and early 20th-century homes use brick and early poured concrete. Each material responds differently to Newport's coastal conditions. Salt crystallization within masonry pores causes surface spalling, while the persistent moisture from Narragansett Bay and Newport Harbor keeps basement walls under constant hydrostatic pressure. Even homes set back from the shoreline face elevated groundwater from the island's limited drainage capacity.

Our techniques are calibrated for historic properties, ensuring that repairs stabilize and seal foundations without damaging the original construction.
