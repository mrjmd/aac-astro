## Foundation Repair Services in Scituate, Massachusetts

From the harbor village to the barrier beach at Humarock, Scituate homeowners contend with environmental forces that few inland communities ever experience. Foundations built during the town's postwar building boom of the 1950s through 1980s are now showing the cumulative effects of decades of coastal weathering.
