## Foundation Repair Services in New London, Connecticut

New London's position on the Thames River and Long Island Sound gives it a maritime heritage stretching back centuries—and foundation challenges that come with coastal living. From the historic homes near Ocean Beach to the dense neighborhoods around Hodges Square, New London's housing stock reflects generations of construction on clay and glacial soils near the water.

Coastal Connecticut foundations face persistent moisture pressure. High water tables push against basement walls, and the salt-laden air accelerates concrete deterioration at the surface. Many New London homeowners first notice water seeping through hairline cracks that have developed over decades of freeze-thaw cycles and hydrostatic pressure from the elevated water table.

Attack A Crack brings over 20 years of foundation repair expertise to New London and eastern Connecticut. Our epoxy and polyurethane injection methods seal cracks from the interior to the exterior soil, stopping water infiltration at its source. Every repair includes our lifetime transferable warranty, giving New London homeowners permanent protection against recurring leaks.
