## Foundation Repair Services in Groton, Connecticut

Groton stretches along the eastern bank of the Thames River to the shores of Long Island Sound, encompassing diverse communities from the submarine base area to the charming coastal villages of Noank and Mystic. The town's housing stock largely dates to the mid-20th century, when Groton's population grew alongside the naval presence at the submarine base.
