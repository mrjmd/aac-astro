## Foundation Repair Services in Norwich, Connecticut

Norwich sits at the confluence of three rivers—the Thames, Yantic, and Shetucket—giving it a rich industrial heritage and a foundation challenge unique to eastern Connecticut. The city's mill-town roots mean many homes in neighborhoods like Taftville and Greeneville date to the early 1900s, with foundations built from poured concrete, block, and even fieldstone.

The river valley soil composition in Norwich creates persistent foundation stress. Clay-heavy deposits retain moisture through wet seasons and shrink during dry periods, pulling away from foundation walls and allowing cracks to form. Connecticut's freeze-thaw cycles then drive water into those cracks, widening them over time. Homeowners in lower-elevation areas near the rivers see these issues most frequently.
