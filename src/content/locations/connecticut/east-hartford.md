## Foundation Repair Services in East Hartford, Connecticut

Sitting across the Connecticut River from Hartford, East Hartford is a working-class community with a housing stock built largely during the town's Pratt & Whitney employment boom of the 1940s through 1960s. These well-maintained homes feature foundations that have now endured 60-80 years of New England weather, and many are showing it.

East Hartford's geography plays a significant role in foundation health. The town occupies the Connecticut River floodplain, meaning many homes sit on alluvial soils with relatively high water tables. Neighborhoods like Silver Lane and Mayberry Village were built on former agricultural land where soil conditions can vary block by block. The Hockanum River, which winds through the eastern part of town, adds another layer of groundwater complexity.

Attack A Crack has been serving East Hartford homeowners since our earliest years in business. We know the specific challenges facing homes in this community—from the thin-wall poured concrete foundations common in the Burnside area to the older block foundations in neighborhoods closer to the river. Our injection process seals cracks permanently from the inside, and our flexible urethane is particularly effective in the wetter soil conditions common throughout East Hartford.
