## Two Different Problems, Two Different Fixes

_Draft: French drains manage general water intrusion along the floor-wall joint. Crack injection seals specific cracks. They solve different things._

## When You Need a French Drain

_Draft: Water seeping along the entire perimeter, rising water table, floor-wall joint seepage, general dampness._

## When You Need Crack Injection

_Draft: Water entering through a specific crack, visible crack in wall, localized leak during rain._

## When You Need Both

_Draft: Common scenario — French drain for perimeter water management plus injection for specific crack leaks._
