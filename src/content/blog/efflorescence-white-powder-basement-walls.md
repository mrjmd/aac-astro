## What Is Efflorescence?

_Draft: Mineral salts carried through concrete by moisture, deposited on the surface when water evaporates._

## Is It Dangerous?

_Draft: Efflorescence itself is harmless. But it indicates moisture is moving through your wall._

## Efflorescence vs. Mold

_Draft: How to tell the difference. Efflorescence is crystalline and white, mold is fuzzy and can be colored._

## What It Tells You About Your Foundation

_Draft: Where you see it = where moisture is entering. Mapping efflorescence patterns._

## Fixing the Underlying Problem

_Draft: Cleaning efflorescence is temporary. Fixing the moisture source is permanent. Crack injection, drainage._
