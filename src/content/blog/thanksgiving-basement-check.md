## You Are Already Down There

_Draft: Light, seasonal hook — you are grabbing the good plates or the extra chairs. While you are in the basement, look around._

## 1. New Cracks or Changed Cracks

_Draft: Quick scan of walls and floor. Anything new since summer? Anything wider?_

## 2. Water Stains or Musty Smell

_Draft: White mineral deposits (efflorescence), dark stains, damp smell = moisture problem._

## 3. Bulkhead Condition

_Draft: Quick check of bulkhead seal, hinges, interior for water signs._

## 4. Humidity Level

_Draft: Condensation on pipes or windows? Feels damp? Note it._

## 5. Sump Pump Test

_Draft: Pour a bucket of water in the pit. Does it turn on? Does it pump?_

## What to Do With What You Find

_Draft: Decision matrix — note it, monitor it, or call us._
