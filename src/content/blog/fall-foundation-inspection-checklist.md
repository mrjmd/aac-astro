## Why Fall Inspections Matter

_Draft: Water in cracks + freezing = expansion = bigger cracks. Fix before winter._

## Exterior Checklist

_Draft: Grading, gutters, downspouts, bulkhead seals, window wells, visible cracks._

## Interior Checklist

_Draft: Basement walls, floor cracks, humidity levels, sump pump test, bulkhead interior._

## The Drainage Check

_Draft: Where is water going when it rains? Downspout extensions, grading slope, French drain condition._

## Scheduling Fall Repairs

_Draft: Timeline for getting repairs done before freeze. Typical booking lead times in October-November._
