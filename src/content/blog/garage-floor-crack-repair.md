## Why Garage Floors Crack

_Draft: Shrinkage during curing, soil settlement, frost heave, heavy vehicle loads, poor drainage._

## Types of Garage Floor Cracks

_Draft: Control joint cracks (normal), shrinkage cracks (cosmetic), settlement cracks (concern), heaving (serious)._

## DIY vs. Professional Repair

_Draft: What homeowners can fill themselves vs. what needs professional attention._

## Professional Repair Methods

_Draft: Epoxy injection for structural, polyurethane for flexible, leveling for settlement._

## Prevention Tips

_Draft: Drainage, sealing, weight distribution, expansion joints._
