## What Is a Crawl Space Foundation?

_Draft: Short foundation walls creating an accessible (or barely accessible) space under the home. Common in older New England homes and additions._

## Common Crawl Space Problems

_Draft: Moisture and standing water, mold growth, pest entry, sagging floor joists, foundation wall cracks._

## Warning Signs From Above

_Draft: Cold floors, musty smells, uneven floors, higher heating bills — signs of crawl space problems you notice from the living space._

## Crawl Space vs. Full Basement

_Draft: Different foundation styles, different challenges. Why crawl spaces are harder to inspect and maintain._

## Repair and Mitigation Options

_Draft: Encapsulation, vapor barriers, crack injection on crawl space walls, drainage, ventilation._
