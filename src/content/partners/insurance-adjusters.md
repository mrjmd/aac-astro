## Partner With Attack A Crack for Foundation Damage Claims

When policyholders file claims involving foundation cracks or water intrusion, you need a repair contractor who delivers quality work, honest assessments, and thorough documentation. That's what we do.

### Why Insurance Adjusters Work With Us

**Accurate, Honest Assessments**
We don't upsell or inflate the scope of work. If a crack is cosmetic and doesn't need repair, we'll tell your policyholder — and you. Our reputation depends on honesty, not maximizing invoices.

**Professional Documentation**
Every repair comes with a complete report including:
- High-resolution before and after photos
- Detailed description of damage found
- Materials and repair method used
- Scope of work performed
- Lifetime warranty certificate

**One-Visit Repairs**
Most foundation crack repairs are completed in a single visit, typically in 2-4 hours. This means less disruption for policyholders and faster claim resolution for you.

### Common Insurance Scenarios We Handle

**Water Intrusion Through Foundation Cracks**
The most common claim we see. Cracks in poured concrete or block foundations allow water into the basement. Our [foundation crack injection](/services/foundation-crack-injection) method seals cracks permanently from the interior — no excavation needed.

**Storm Damage Follow-Up**
Heavy rain events often reveal existing foundation vulnerabilities. For structural concerns, we offer [carbon fiber reinforcement](/services/carbon-fiber-stitches) in addition to crack injection. We assess whether damage is storm-related or pre-existing and provide clear documentation either way.

**Pre-Sale Inspection Findings**
When a home sale triggers an inspection that reveals foundation issues, we provide rapid assessment and repair to keep the transaction on track.
