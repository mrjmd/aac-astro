## Foundation Repair Execution for Structural Engineers

You diagnose the problem. We fix it. When your assessment calls for crack injection, carbon fiber reinforcement, or bulkhead sealing, partner with specialists who execute your recommendations with precision.

### Why Engineers Partner With Us

**We Follow Your Specs**
When you write a repair plan, you need it followed. We work from your specifications and use materials and methods that meet your engineering requirements. If we have questions, we ask before we start—not after.

**Specialized Equipment & Materials**
Foundation injection isn't general contracting. It requires specialized pumps, ports, resins, and carbon fiber systems. We've invested in professional-grade equipment and maintain it meticulously.

**Daily Expertise**
Our technicians perform [foundation crack injection](/services/foundation-crack-injection) and [carbon fiber reinforcement](/services/carbon-fiber-stitches) every day. That repetition means consistent results your clients can count on.

### Common Engineer-Specified Repairs We Handle

**Crack Injection**
Epoxy and urethane injection for structural and waterproofing repairs. We match resin type and injection pressure to your specifications—whether it's structural epoxy for load-bearing walls or flexible urethane for active water leaks.

**Carbon Fiber Reinforcement**
[Carbon fiber stitches and straps](/services/carbon-fiber-stitches) for bowing walls and structural crack stabilization. We install to manufacturer specifications and provide tensile strength documentation.

**Bulkhead Sealing**
[Bulkhead joint sealing](/services/leaky-bulkhead-repair) where precast meets poured foundation. Permanent urethane injection that eliminates water infiltration at the joint.

**Utility Line Penetrations**
[Sewer, well, and conduit line sealing](/services/sewer-well-conduit-line-repair) where pipes pass through foundation walls. Flexible urethane fills the gap permanently without excavation.
