## Partner With Attack A Crack: Concrete Repair for Your Clients

As a landscaper, you work on properties every day where concrete is cracked, heaved, or deteriorating. Driveways, patios, walkways, pool decks — homeowners ask you about these problems constantly. Now you have a trusted partner to handle the concrete work.

### Why Landscapers Partner With Us

**Concrete Problems Are Everywhere**
Every property you work on has concrete. And New England's freeze-thaw cycles, tree roots, and settling soils mean that concrete is constantly cracking and failing. Your clients notice — and they ask you about it.

**Exterior Concrete (MA)**
Our Massachusetts team specializes in repairing and resurfacing:

- Deteriorating patios

- Pool deck repairs
- Garage floor cracks
- Stairway repairs

**Foundation Repairs (CT + MA)**
Throughout Connecticut and Massachusetts, we handle:
- Foundation wall crack injection

### The Landscaper-Foundation Connection

**Grading and Drainage**
When you're regrading a property or improving drainage, that's often because the homeowner has water in their basement. The exterior drainage fix addresses one half of the problem — we address the other by sealing the actual cracks in the foundation.

**Tree Root Damage**
Tree roots don't just damage concrete surfaces. They can crack foundation walls and disrupt basement slabs. When you're removing problem trees or roots, let us assess any foundation damage.

**Seasonal Patterns**
Spring thaw and fall rains drive both your busiest seasons and ours. Coordinate referrals during these peak times to serve clients when they need it most.
